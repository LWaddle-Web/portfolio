Hi! My name is Ally Waddle!

Creator/Designer/Photographer

For years, I have been following bloggers, pinning things to boards on Pinterest, and building up a considerable collection of skills and equipment. All the while dreaming and trying to figure out what I wanted to do with all the things floating around my brain.

Waddle We Create Designs is the realization of a long time dream!

Growing up, I attended an elementary school focused on the arts, and I loved it! My childhood days were spent painting, singing, performing skits, and dancing. My fondest memories included finger stretches before art class, vocal warmups before chorus and drama class, and running a bow along the strings of the violin. We moved away from that school, but I never lost my love for creating.

As a stay at home and then homeschool mom, a lot of my time in the past 16 years has been spent learning how to live on a budget, making money in creative ways, and teaching my kids as best I can while I learn along side them. I have been attending Wake Tech Community College as a part time student for the past 4 years, double majoring in Accounting and Graphic Design working towards this dream! I want to create, design, and share what I have been learning. I have been attending classes along with continuing to homeschool my kids, working part time, running my house (that is also a fixer upper), and trying to fit in my love for traveling wherever I can. Basically, my brain rarely takes a break.
